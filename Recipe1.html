Pizza

Ingredients:

  • package (about 2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cups warm water (110째F/45째C)
  • 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • tablespoons olive oil
  • teaspoon salt
  • Your choice of pizza sauce
  • Your choice of toppings (e.g., shredded mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.)

Instructions: In a small bowl, dissolve the sugar in warm water. Sprinkle the yeast over the water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a soft dough forms. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough is too sticky, you can add a little more flour as needed. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until it doubles in size. Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C) and grease a pizza pan or baking sheet. Punch down the risen dough and transfer it to the prepared pan. Use your hands to press it out into a round shape, about 12-14 inches in diameter, or to fit your pan. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your desired toppings. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Cooking Time:

  • Preparing the dough: 1-2 hours (including rising time)
  • Baking the pizza: 12-15 minutes


Enjoy your homemade pizza!
